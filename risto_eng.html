Agriturisms near the house

Stazzu Li Paladini

Just 5 minutes from the residence, it offers regional cuisine with a fixed menu to taste specialties like porceddu, seadas, homemade myrtle, and much more.

Agriturismo San Giovanni

12 minutes from the residence, it offers abundant cuisine typical of Gallura, featuring dishes like ravioli, gnocchetti, and Gallurese soup.

Restaurants near Olbia

Bacchus

Located 20 minutes from the house, Bacchus is a Michelin-referred restaurant offering a seasonal menu. Excellent, abundant, and diverse. Prices are reasonable for this type of restaurant. Request a table with a pool view for a perfect experience.

Da Bartolo

Located near the center of Olbia, easily reachable by car, this restaurant surprises with its intimate atmosphere, friendly staff, and typical and refined Sardinian cuisine.

Megastores

Spazio Conad

In the Terranova shopping center, you'll find the largest supermarket in the area. You'll find everything you need, including cheeses and other typical Sardinian or gastronomic products.

Muraja

In case you need a beach umbrella, a stake, or any other item that comes to mind, you will surely find it at a low price at Muraja. Ample parking available.

Club

Ritual

A captivating nightclub immersed in an ancient temple. A unique experience accompanied by top-level DJs (elegant dress code).

Phi Beach

Outdoor venue with DJ sets and breathtaking views to enjoy the sunset. During tourist seasons, an entrance fee may be required.

Vesper Beach Club

Just a stone's throw from one of the most famous beaches of the Costa Smeralda (Capriccioli), the perfect place for an elegant aperitif or an evening accompanied by exceptional DJs.

Country Club Porto Rotondo

If you are looking for a standard nightclub, this venue has made history in Italian nightlife. Check their Instagram page to discover the themed nights that await you.
